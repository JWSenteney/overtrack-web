Streamer Info

If you are a streamer (or content creator) and would like to use OverTrack to provide details of your games to viewers then this is the page for you.

Free OverTrack for partnered streamers

If you are a partnered streamer then I am happy to upgrade your account to track full game details for free.
Just join the OverTrack Discord and ping me (@Muon) with your battletag you signed up to OverTrack with and your Twitch handle.

You can still choose to subscribe at any time if you think that OverTrack is worth supporting.


Custom share link

If you would like a custom share link to your games (instead of the autogenerated battletag-<random string> from the "Share" button) I can generate a custom share link matching your twitch handle.
e.g. https://overtrack.gg/games/eeveea.

Share links can also be configured to only show specific accounts (while the autogenerated link will display all accounts).
You may also request multiple share links, for example if you want a separate link for different accounts.


Bot commands

OverTrack can be used to provide details about your games upon request in chat. Currently the supported commands are:

  • Current SR
  • Last Match
To use these commands you will need a bot that supports fetching from a URL. For Nightbot this is the $(urlfetch ...) syntax.
Configure your bot to respond to a command using the content from one of OverTrack's bot APIs.

Current SR

To retrieve current SR use the endpoint https://api.overtrack.gg/sr/<key>.
For example if your share link is https://overtrack.gg/games/eeveea then your share key is eeveea, so your SR endpoint is https://api.overtrack.gg/sr/eeveea.
