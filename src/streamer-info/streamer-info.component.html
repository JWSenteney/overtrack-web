Streamer Info

If you are a streamer or content creator you may wish to use some of OverTrackâ€™s integrations as additional ways for your viewers to engage with your stream.

With OverTrack you can create chat commands that allow your viewers to request information from your most recent matches. OverTrack also offers a twitch bot and a discord bot which can post each match you have completed to a discord channel.


Twitch Bot

Link your twitch account in the "Integrations" panel on your games list and enable the twitch bot to post after your matches. Additionally this will let you use your twitch account name as a share link (enable this in the "Share Links" panel).


Bot commands

OverTrack can be used to provide details about your games using chat commands. Currently, the supported commands are:

  • Current SR
  • Last Match
  • Session

To use these commands you will need a bot that supports fetching from a URL.
For Nightbot this is the $(urlfetch ...) syntax and for Ankhbot $readapi(...).
You will need to configure your bot to respond to a command of your choice using the content from one of OverTrack's bot APIs.



Current SR

To retrieve current SR use the endpoint https://api.overtrack.gg/sr/<share key>.
For example if your share link is https://overtrack.gg/games/SHAREKEY123 then your share key is SHAREKEY123, so your SR endpoint is https://api.overtrack.gg/sr/SHAREKEY123.

Session

To retrieve a summary of the wins, losses, and draws along with SR change of your current session of tames use the endpoint https://api.overtrack.gg/current_session/<share key>.
