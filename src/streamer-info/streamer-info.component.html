Free OverTrack for partnered streamers

If you are a partnered streamer then I am happy to upgrade your account to track full game details for free.
Just join the OverTrack Discord and ping me (@Muon) with the BattleTag you signed up to OverTrack with and your Twitch handle.

You can still choose to subscribe at any time if you think that OverTrack is worth supporting.


Custom share link

If you would like a custom share link to your games (instead of the autogenerated BattleTag-<random string> from the "Share" button) I can generate a custom share link matching your twitch handle. e.g. https://overtrack.gg/games/eeveea.

Custom share links can also be configured to only show specific accounts, as opposed to the default behavior of the autogenerated links, which will display games from all accounts linked to your OverTrack profile.

You may also request multiple share links - for example, if you want a separate link for different accounts.
If you would like a custom share link to your games (instead of the autogenerated BattleTag-<random string> from the "Share" button) shoot me (@Muon) a message in the OverTrack Discord.


Bot commands

OverTrack can be used to provide details about your games using chat commands. Currently, the supported commands are:

  • Current SR
  • Last Match
Although more are planned and suggestions ar welcome.

To use these commands you will need a bot that supports fetching from a URL.
For Nightbot this is the $(urlfetch ...) syntax and for Ankhbot $readapi(...).
You will need to configure your bot to respond to a command of your choice using the content from one of OverTrack's bot APIs.


Current SR

To retrieve current SR use the endpoint https://api.overtrack.gg/sr/<key>.
For example if your share link is https://overtrack.gg/games/eeveea then your share key is eeveea, so your SR endpoint is https://api.overtrack.gg/sr/eeveea.
