Statistics

Here are the player statistics

From statistics.component.ts we have access (in this templated HTML) to gamesLists

gamesLists is an array of GamesListEntry (see games-list/games-list.service.ts). Most people will only have one element in the array - more than one means they have multiple accounts. In order to track stats on multiple accounts we will probably want to use separate tabs (like in the games list).
